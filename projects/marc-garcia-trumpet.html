Marc Garcia Trumpet

A solo-built, responsive website for a St. Louis trumpet performer, recording artist, adjudicator, and clinician — designed, developed, deployed, and kept running end to end.

View live site ↗

The Problem

A professional trumpet performer and educator needed an online presence that matched the mood of his work — something that felt like a performance, not a template — while still being easy to keep current and simple to reach him through.

How I Solved It

  • Designed, developed, deployed, and maintained the site solo, start to finish
  • Built a responsive layout that holds up across phones, tablets, and desktop
  • Managed hosting and domain configuration for the live site
  • Tested functionality across pages and devices after every update

Technologies

  • HTML/CSS/JS
  • Audio Embeds
  • Responsive Design
  • Hosting & DNS

What I Learned

How to carry a client's tone and personality through every design decision — and what it takes to own a site completely, from the first line of code to the domain renewal.
